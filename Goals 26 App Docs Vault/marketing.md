# Solas Run -- Marketing

> **Your Personal Operating System**

---

## Elevator Pitch

Solas Run is a personal operating system that brings business-grade discipline to your personal goals. It unifies goal tracking, project management, habit building, journaling, meeting intelligence, and AI coaching into one self-hosted application -- so you can run your life with the same rigor you give your best clients.

---

## The Problem

Knowledge workers are drowning in fragmentation.

- **Too many tools.** Goals live in one app, tasks in another, habits in a third, journals in a fourth. Context is scattered across a dozen tabs and nothing connects.
- **No accountability for personal goals.** Client deadlines get met. Personal ambitions get pushed to "someday." There is no status meeting for your health, no sprint review for your creative work.
- **Deferred work disappears.** Ideas get captured in notes that are never reviewed. Opportunities pass because there is no system to resurface them at the right time.
- **No holistic view.** Even the most organized professionals rarely have a single dashboard that shows progress across all the dimensions that matter -- career, health, relationships, finances, growth, and creativity.

The result: talented people consistently underperform in the areas of life that matter most to them.

---

## The Solution

Solas Run is a single, unified system that replaces the patchwork of disconnected productivity tools with one coherent operating layer for your life.

It combines:

- **Goal and project management** with hierarchical task decomposition
- **Habit tracking** with streaks, rings, and trend analysis
- **Daily planning** with AI-generated time blocks
- **Journaling** with mood tracking and Life Area linkage
- **Meeting intelligence** that extracts action items and business signals from transcripts
- **AI coaching** at eight integration points across the daily and weekly workflow
- **An opportunity pipeline** that ensures nothing you defer is ever truly lost

All of it is organized under six Life Areas that keep you balanced, and presented through a dashboard with nine widgets that give you complete situational awareness every morning.

---

## Key Differentiators

### 1. Self-as-Client Model

Most people give their best planning and follow-through to work they do for others. Solas Run flips this pattern. It treats you as your own most important client, with the same project plans, status reviews, and accountability structures you would use for a paying engagement.

### 2. GTD Deferral Pipeline

When you defer something, it does not vanish into a forgotten list. Solas Run captures deferred work and moves it through a structured pipeline that tracks commercial value, sets review dates, and surfaces opportunities when the timing is right. Conscious deferral becomes a strategic tool, not an admission of failure.

### 3. Hierarchical Task Decomposition

Goals are only useful when they are broken down into actionable work. Solas Run provides AI-guided task decomposition that turns high-level ambitions into tree-structured task hierarchies with quality gates at each level. Progress rolls up automatically from leaf tasks to parent goals.

### 4. Meeting Intelligence

Through integration with the Granola MCP server, Solas Run automatically extracts action items, scope decisions, resource signals, and sentiment indicators from meeting transcripts. No more losing critical follow-ups in a sea of notes.

### 5. Life Area Balance

Six core Life Areas -- Creative, Business, Health, Family, Growth, and Finance -- ensure that progress is measured holistically. The dashboard makes imbalance visible immediately, preventing the common pattern of over-indexing on work at the expense of everything else.

### 6. Dashboard as Daily Command Center

Nine purpose-built widgets give you a complete picture of your day and your trajectory the moment you open the application. Day theme, time blocks, habit rings, goal progress, streaks, AI intentions, and the opportunity pipeline -- all in one view.

## Target Audience

Solas Run is built for people who take their personal effectiveness seriously:

- **Freelancers** managing multiple clients alongside personal goals
- **Solopreneurs** who need to balance business building with life balance
- **Consultants** juggling complex client engagements and professional development
- **Creative professionals** who need structure without rigidity
- **Knowledge workers** who have outgrown consumer productivity apps

The common thread: these are people who know how to run a project for someone else and want that same capability turned inward.

## Use Cases

### Freelance Business Management
Track client projects, extract meeting action items, manage the opportunity pipeline, and ensure business goals get the same weekly review as billable work.

### Personal Development Tracking
Set growth goals, decompose them into learning paths, build daily study habits, and journal about progress -- all connected under the Growth Life Area.

### Client Project Delivery
Manage deliverables with hierarchical task trees, track scope decisions from meeting transcripts, and surface deferred items before they become missed deadlines.

### Habit Building and Health Goals
Define health habits with daily tracking, visualize streaks, monitor trends over time, and link health outcomes to journal reflections.

### Financial Planning and Tracking
Set financial goals under the Finance Life Area, break them into quarterly milestones, and review progress in structured weekly reviews.

### Creative Project Management
Organize creative work under the Creative Life Area with project plans, task decomposition, and time blocks dedicated to creative output.

## Brand Values

### Sovereignty
Solas Run is self-hosted. Your data, your rules. The name itself -- **Solas** (Light) and **Run** (Secret/Mystery) in Irish -- speaks to illuminating what is hidden: the patterns of how you spend your time, the goals you quietly carry, the potential you have not yet activated.

### Discipline
Structure is not the enemy of creativity. Discipline is what turns ambition into outcomes. Solas Run provides the frameworks, reviews, and accountability loops that make consistent progress possible.

### Balance
Six Life Areas exist because no single dimension of life is sufficient. Business success without health is hollow. Creative output without relationships is isolating. Solas Run keeps the whole picture visible.

### Growth
The system is designed around the assumption that you are not finished becoming who you want to be. Every feature -- from AI-guided goal brainstorming to weekly reviews to the opportunity pipeline -- is oriented toward forward motion.
